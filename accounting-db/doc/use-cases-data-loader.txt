Use Cases for Data Loader

Agent: DBA

Load existing accounting data into new database for a single entity.

1. Read data from annual accounting dump directories tab-delimited files for all years for an entity.
   a. Transactions and items
   b. Accounts and account groups
   c. Balances from initial year
   d. AR and AR reimbursement transactions

2. Write data into new database for the entity.
   a. Entity
   b. Account Group and Account
   c. Fiscal Year and Fiscal Year Account
   d. Transactions and Items
